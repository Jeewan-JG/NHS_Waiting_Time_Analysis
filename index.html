NHS Waiting Times Analysis

Volume, Delays & Provider Performance Analysis using Python & Tableau

Project Overview

This project analyzes NHS waiting list data to understand the scale of long waiting times, identify the drivers of the 52+ week backlog, and compare provider performance.

  • Total patient volume and long-wait backlog
  • Percentage of patients waiting over 52 weeks
  • Monthly changes in waiting performance
  • Differences in provider performance
  • Relationship between volume and waiting times
  • Distribution of long waiting times

📊 Dashboard

The analysis is visualized using Tableau dashboards combining KPIs, trends, provider comparisons, and waiting time distributions.

  • Total patients & 52+ week KPIs
  • Trend over time
  • Provider comparison
  • Volume vs waiting time relationship

📈 Key Insights

  • Significant backlog across the NHS
  • Higher volume leads to longer waits
  • Large variation between providers
  • Recent improvements are unstable

Business Recommendations

  • Redistribute workload across providers
  • Focus on high wait-ratio providers
  • Improve triage and digital services
  • Prioritise 52+ week backlog reduction
